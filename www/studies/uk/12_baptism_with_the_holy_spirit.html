Вступ: Jesus was given the Spirit in full measure (RSV), no limit ( Івана 3:34)  There are three measures of the Holy Spirit:
  1. The Indwelling of the Holy Spirit
    1. Terminology from Римлянам 8:9 (RSV)
    2. Отримуємо при хрещенні  ( Дії 2:38 )
  2. Хрещення Святим Духом
    1. Опис в Дії 2 і Дії 10
      1. Обіцянка (не заповідь) Дії 1:4-5
      2. Predicted (prophesied)
      3. Came without warning. (People were not specifically praying for it)
      4. Мови
      5. Purpose: to usher in the Kingdom with power  ( Дії 1:8 )
    2. Accounts of the Baptism with the Holy Spirit
      1. To the Jews — in Jerusalem  ( Дії 2 )
      2. To the Gentiles — begins with Cornelius  ( Дії 10 )
        1. Note: Cornelius and his household were water baptized in Дії 10:48 ,  saved at baptism
        2. Peter explained actions to the Jews  ( Дії 11:1-18 )
        3. "At the beginning"  ( Дії 11:15 )
        4. Peter gives the "keys to the Kingdom" to both Jews and Gentiles
    3. Does the Baptism with the Holy Spirit still exist today?  Єфесянам 4:4-6 :  There is one baptism — which one?  (Written about  60-62 р. н.е.)  There are three options:
      1. John's baptism — Passed when the new covenant began  ( Acts 19:1-5 )
      2. The Baptism with the Holy Spirit  ( Дії 2 і Дії 10 ) —  is no longer present as it was a prophecy/promise that has been fulfilled. It was never a general command for all Christians
      3. Baptism with water in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of sins to receive the indwelling of the Holy Spirit
        1. Jesus commanded this baptism  ( Матвія 28:18-20 )
        2. This baptism is recorded all the way through the book of Acts and the Epistles. In fact, Peter was present for all the baptisms: John's baptism, both accounts of the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, and preached water baptism in the name of Jesus in Acts 2  33 р. н.е.) і в 1 Peter 3:18-21 (written around 64 р. н.е.),  well after all the events of the book of Acts
        3. It had to be the one baptism of  Єфесянам 4:4-6 as it was the only one practiced by 60-64 р. н.е. when Ephesians was written
  3. The miraculous gifts of the Holy Spirit (next lesson)
    1. Received by the apostles' laying on of hands
    2. No longer present today
